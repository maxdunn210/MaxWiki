About InsMark
 

The Company
For over 20 years, InsMark has been the leader in sales presentation software and related marketing services for the insurance and financial services industry. We maintain our market edge because of the uniqueness of the innovative marketing strategies contained within our various software systems.

Located in San Ramon, CA (a San Francisco suburb), InsMark provides a healthy, safe and professional work environment with state of the art tools. We provide a flexible work environment and strive to offer an attractive array of employee benefits. Some of the benefits for full-time employees include:

    • 8 Paid Holidays
    • Medical and Dental Coverage
    • Group Life Insurance
    • Long Term Disability Insurance
    • 401 (K)
    • Paid Vacation
    • Direct Deposit
    • 6 days Paid Sick Leave
    • Optional Compressed Schedule (every other Friday off)*
    • Pre-Paid Legal Benefit
    • Credit Union
    • Flexible schedule*
    • 7.5 hour workday

*Applies to most positions

The InsMark Team
The comment heard most often from our visitors is that InsMark seems like a great place to work. We agree! At InsMark we value the individual, and each employee has an opportunity to make a difference. You are part of a team rather than a number!

The Team has a lot of energy and constantly finds more efficient ways to do business and better ways to improve our customer service. Many of our employees have been with InsMark for 10 to 15 years – now that tells you something!
