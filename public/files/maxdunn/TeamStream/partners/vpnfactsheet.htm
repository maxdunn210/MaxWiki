Overview: TeamStream, Inc develops, customizes and licenses Internet collaboration infrastructure software to selected strategic partners. TeamStream's solutions enable virtual teams to work together easily and securely, no matter where in the world the team members are located. TeamDrive, the company's proprietary flagship software product, turns the Internet into a virtual hard-drive to provide fast, easy and secure Internet collaboration. For many small teams, this provides all of the functionality they need from a VPN but has the advantage that it is much easier to setup and use.

Company: TeamStream is focused on developing Internet collaboration applications and then customizing them for integration into our partner's product lines. TeamStream's main strength is its development team located in the Czech Republic where development costs are low and development quality is high.

Next Generation VPN:. In the past, VPNs were usually the best way to connect offices or telecommuters. However, the world is moving forward and for some teams - especially workgroups, small business and small remote offices - there may be better solutions. Many team members don't need to have access to all the resources of the corporate LAN but instead just want a solution to share documents that is easy to setup and use. For these users, TeamDrive can be a better solution.

TeamDrive: TeamDrive provides a central Internet location to securely share documents without the risk of opening up the corporate LAN to outsiders. Any user with an Internet connection can easily and quickly install and use TeamDrive. TeamDrive encrypts data packets just like a normal VPN to insure that the data is always protected. TeamDrive also speeds access to documents by providing compression, local caching and delta transfer technology. TeamDrive's flexible security and sharing options allow administrators or users to control access to documents.

Customization: There are several different options for deploying TeamDrive.

  • TeamDrive can be used as-is with many WebDAV, FTP, HTTP and TeamDrive servers
  • TeamDrive can be co-branded
  • TeamDrive can be customized with additional features
  • TeamDrive can be renamed so all references to TeamDrive are replaced with our partner's desired product name
  • TeamDrive's functionality can be incorporated into existing products

TeamDrive Client Features

  • Fast: Intelligent caching and background transfers make users think they are working with a local drive
  • Easy: Uses a mapped drive-letter so all programs can open documents and edit them in-place on the server
  • Flexible: Works with WebDAV, FTP, HTTP, TeamDrive and proprietary servers
  • Customizable: Can be extended to support other advanced features
  • Co-Branded: Includes partners graphics and preset with partners service information
  • Technical: DLL and VxD installed on client computer, 800k download size
  • Platforms: Win9x, WinME, WinNT, Win2000, WinXP
TeamDrive Server Features
  • Secure: All logins are authenticated and transfers are encrypted
  • Fast: Transfers are compressed for the fastest possible throughput
  • Collaborative: Allows documents to be shared and locked
  • Easy administration: Does not require administrators; if allowed, users can signup themselves and share documents with others
  • Remote Management: Can be remotely managed through a web browser
  • Technical: 1.5Mb download size
  • Platforms: Win9x, WinME, WinNT, Win2000, WinXP, Linux
Advantages of TeamDrive
  • Provides 80% of VPN capabilities
  • Easier to setup and maintain
  • Allows spontaneous collaboration
  • Perfect for workgroups, small companies or telecommuters
