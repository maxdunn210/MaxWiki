TEAMSTREAM ANNOUNCES UNIVERSAL DRIVE-LETTER ACCESS TO ONLINE STORAGE SERVICES

TeamDrive turns Driveway, xDrive, i-drive, MySpace and others into virtual Internet drives

San Jose, CA - January 26, 2001 - TeamStream, Inc. today announced the release of its free universal TeamDrive client that makes it faster and easier to access online storage services (OSSs) such as Driveway, xDrive, i-drive, MySpace and others. TeamDrive works by mapping a drive-letter across the Internet to turn the OSS into a virtual drive. This virtual drive can then be used like any other drive including using Windows Explorer to copy and manage files or using any program to open and edit files directly in-place.

"The first generation of OSSs required users to upload and download files manually through a browser interface which was awkward," reports Max Dunn, president and CEO of TeamStream. "The next generation of OSSs improved on this by offering drag-and-drop file transfers. TeamDrive takes online storage services to the next level by turning them into true virtual drives."

TeamDrive is the first product to provide a universal drive-letter interface to many different types of OSSs. TeamDrive uses a variety of techniques including background transfers and intelligent caching to make users feel like they are using a normal drive, even on slower dial-up connections. TeamDrive also makes it easy to signup for OSS accounts or to be connected to several OSSs at the same time.

Online Storage Service (OSS) Background

There are over 60 OSSs that provide free or paid storage of files over the Internet. They are useful for accessing files from work and home, sharing files with others, backing up files or providing more storage space. A list of OSSs and their capabilities can be found at www.maxdunn.com/teamstream/oss.

Pricing and Availability

TeamDrive is free and can be downloaded from www.TeamDrive.com. TeamDrive is available for Windows 95, 98 and Me. Support for Windows NT and 2000 will be available soon.

TeamDrive currently supports Driveway, i-drive, xDrive, MySpace, IBackup, My Docs Online, RealFTP, Sharemation, Globedesk and e-ttach. Other services are added frequently upon request.
