IBackup to Offer TeamDrive to Its Customers Through Partnership with TeamStream

San Jose, CA, September 11, 2000 — TeamStream, Inc., a global developer of Internet collaboration infrastructure products enabling virtual teams to work together smoothly and securely, today announced a partnership with IBackup ( a division of Pro Softnet Corporation) an open, highly customizable fast data storage, access and data sharing solution on the web.

"Implementation of Network Drive technology is a key addition to ibackup's many ways to manipulate user's data. IBackup, with its efficient storage infrastructure, will be able to offer a fast Network Drive solution to its users by partnering with Teamstream Inc. " said Raghu Kulkarni, President and CEO of Pro Softnet Corporation.

Through the partnership, clients of IBackup can offer TeamDrive to their customers, allowing them to directly access their IBackup accounts. IBackup users can map a drive letter to their account so they can use Windows Explorer or Windows Commander to drag and drop files back and forth between their computer and the IBackup account. Editing is done directly on the IBackup server so no copying to the local computer is needed.

"TeamStream has always possessed a strong commitment to technology and we are very excited to be working with a company that believes in offering its customers the most advanced solutions available," said Rick C. Garian, President and CEO of TeamStream. "This partnership will allow IBackup clients to offer their customers a product that's quickly becoming a necessity in the online storage market."

About IBackup

IBackup (www.ibackup.com) is a service of Pro Softnet Corporation, a software consulting and Internet Services company based in Woodland Hills, CA. IBackup is an open, highly customizable data storage, access and data sharing solution with several possible interfaces and options to backup/retrieve and manipulate your data on the web.
