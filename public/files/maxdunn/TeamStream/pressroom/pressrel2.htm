TeamStream Elects Chief Business Officer of SciClone to its Board

SAN JOSE, California, June 28 /PRNewswire/ -- TeamStream, an Internet collaboration infrastructure software start-up, has elected Shawn K. Singh to its Board of Directors. Singh is Chief Business Officer of SciClone Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (NASDAQ: SCLN), an international biopharmaceutical company. Prior to SciClone, Singh was the Corporate Finance Associate for the Silicon Valley office of Morrison & Foerster where he specialized in counseling start-ups on formation, mergers and acquisitions, strategic partnerships, venture capital and initial public offerings.

“We are pleased that TeamStream is able to attract an individual of this quality and experience. Shawn’s extensive and diverse background in corporate finance and global commercial operations will add substantial depth to our rapidly expanding business”, said Rick C. Garian, President and CEO of TeamStream.

Strategic meetings are now underway with Online Storage Services (OSSs) Customer partners, helping them provide the best service possible for their users.

About TeamStream

TeamStream, Inc. develops Internet collaboration infrastructure products enabling virtual teams to work together smoothly and securely. Whether its customers are business or home PC users, TeamStream has the right tools available that easily collaborate effectively via the Internet. TeamStream’s 1st product TeamDrive provides a suite of solutions that allows virtual teams to work together quickly, easily and securely over the Internet. The technology is applicable for Online Storage Services (OSS), Virtual Private Networks (VPNs), Application Service Providers (ASPs), Enterprise Information Portals (EIPs) and Internet Service Providers (ISPs). With TeamStream's products, people no longer need to transfer their work to a CD or floppy disk.
