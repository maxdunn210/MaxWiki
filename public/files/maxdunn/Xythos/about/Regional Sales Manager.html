Regional Sales Manager

Description

The regional sales manager is responsible for establishing, developing, and managing relationships to maximize sales opportunities and customer satisfaction. This individual must be customer-focused, able to execute on and meet or exceed quarterly and annual sales targets.

Responsible for regional sales & management of regional reseller relations for software sales to enterprise clients. Build and manage the regional pipeline and forecast, including developing and executing area account plans and allocating appropriate sales engineering resources. Responsible for all aspects of the sales process from prospect qualification to price negotiation and closing. Work with marketing to optimize regional lead generation efforts.

Requirements

Consistent record of revenue production - both as an individual and/or as a manager. Demonstrated success of selling product for early stage companies and/or early adoption of new technologies. Experienced in working in a start-up environment with demonstrated track record of success finding and establishing new opportunities. Previous experience selling: Content Management Systems, Web Infrastructure, or enterprise infrastructure software. Demonstrated skill to influence executives through effective presentation, term negotiation, and solution selling (presenting total cost of ownership and ROI vs. alternatives). Strong verbal and written communications skills. Minimum of 8 years experience in selling enterprise software. Bachelor's degree or equivalent is preferred.

Also required is possession of excellent business acumen, ability to demonstrate ethical behavior, results focus, vision, passion, confidence, persistence and commitment.

Compensation

Competitive+, outstanding benefit package

Location

Various - US

Sales
