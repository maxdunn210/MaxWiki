Product Manager - Software as a Service (SaaS)

Description

We are looking for a product manager for the software as a service (SaaS) side of our business. The right person for this job will have the right mix of experience, enthusiasm, organization, and creativity. You will be helping take a new product to the next stage in its development, as an important new part of an already established business.

This position reports directly to the Vice President of Product Management, and will have significant visibility inside and outside the company.

Responsibilities

  • Research and write product requirements.
  • Work directly with the development team.
  • Work directly with customers and partners.
  • Collaborate with other product managers who work on the on-premise versions of our technologies.
  • Handle project management issues related to our SaaS business.
  • Support the marketing, sales, and business development organizations.

Essential Job Functions

  • At least 2-3 years in product management.

  • Knowledge of the SaaS market.
  • Experience in a SaaS business.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills.
  • Project management skills.
  • Record of professional presentations.
  • Ability to work in the United States.
  • Experience with desktop and web-based productivity applications.
  • Knowledge of Web 2.0 technologies.
  • Knowledge of important desktop and web technologies.

Desirable

  • Experience with collaboration or document management software.
  • Experience crafting vertical product offerings.
  • Experience with solutions designed for particular verticals.
  • Hands-on experience building desktop and web applications, including Web 2.0 technologies.
  • Experience with Java, XML, SQL, WebDAV, and LDAP.

Compensation

Competitive+, outstanding benefit package

Location

San Francisco, CA
