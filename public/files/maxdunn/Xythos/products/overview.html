Xythos product suites are comprehensive software solutions for securely managing and sharing data and content across the entire enterprise. What truly distinguishes Xythos products from the competition is their platform neutrality and ease of integration. Each Xythos product is designed from the beginning to leverage open standards-based technologies making it easy to deploy in any environment either as a stand-alone solution or as a content management component of other applications and systems. Xythos product suites can be enhanced or extended with additional Xythos products and can be easily customized using the Xythos Developer Studio, Xythos API's and a comprehensive set of JavaDocs.

Enterprise Document Management Suite

Xythos Enterprise Document Management Suite is the cost-effective alternative to traditional ECM systems and includes everything distributed organizations need to safely access, manage and share content over the web. Xythos helps organizations take advantage of Web 2.0 technology with Ajax application interfaces and integrated support for Wiki’s and RSS. Content, collaboration and context can be captured and stored in a single environment for classification, tagging and re-use. Email and document retention can be managed within the same system providing organizations with an attractive and comprehensive solution for all unstructured content.

Digital Locker Suite

Xythos Digital Locker Suite rises to today’s campus collaboration challenge with everything a campus community needs to manage and share content on the web. Designed to work together common desktop applications through the ease of its new Web 2.0 interface Xythos Digital Locker helps accelerate user adoption, improves compliance and reduces support requirements. Integrated social networking tools including Wikis, RSS and file tagging enhance collaboration options while process wizards simplify content organization.

WebFile Development Suite

Xythos WebFile Development Suite is the complete set of products for developers to cost-effectively integrate document and file management functionality into enterprise applications.
